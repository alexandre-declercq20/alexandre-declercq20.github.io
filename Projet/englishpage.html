Why are we doing this?

We thought about creating the TPJN because we're students who want to challenge and play with new people.The 3 of us used to play pétanque together, but we couldn't find anyone around us who wanted to play with us.

So we started looking on the internet to find people to play with. After several searches, we came to the conclusion that there are a large number of people who want to play pétanque for fun or competitively but can't find anyone else to play with.

This is how we came up with the idea of creating the TPJN, which will be a way of bringing together beginners and experienced players to come and play pétanque with others.
